About Us

Welcome to Brilliance Technologies – Your Database & Web Solutions Experts

Brilliance Technologies is a proudly Irish company, dedicated to delivering expert database and web solutions to businesses across Ireland and beyond. With a team of seasoned Oracle, PostgreSQL, and Microsoft SQL Server professionals, as well as talented web designers, we provide a comprehensive range of services to meet your business’s technology needs.
